### **For SIGN IN (Existing User):**
1. **Email field**: Type your email
   - Example: `ahmed@gmail.com`
2. **Password field**: Type your password
   - Example: `password123`
3. Click **"Sign In"** button
4. Done! ✅

### **For SIGN UP (New User):**
1. **Your Name**: Type your name
   - Example: `Ahmed` or `John` or `Fatima`

   - Example: `ahmed@gmail.com`
3. **Password**: Choose a password (minimum 6 characters)

5. Done! ✅
