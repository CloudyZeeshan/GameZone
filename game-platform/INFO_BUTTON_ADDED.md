### **Header:**

- 🏆 **Save Your Scores** - Your high scores are saved automatically
- 📊 **Track Progress** - See your improvement over time
- 👤 **Personal Dashboard** - Your own profile and game history
- 🎯 **Compete** - Challenge yourself to beat your best

1. Click **"Sign In"** button (top right)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** link at bottom
3. **Enter** your name, email, and password
4. Click **"Sign Up"** button - Done! 🎉

### **Section 3: Install as App (Optional)**
Install GameZone on your device for the best experience:
- ✅ Play games instantly
- ✅ Works offline
- ✅ Full-screen mode (no browser)
- ✅ Looks like a native app

💡 Look for the "Install App" button at the bottom of the page!
